--- number: 5 name: Software Engineering Project Management type: Non-core slug: software-engineering-project-management start_date: November 2021 end_date: February 2022 tutor: Douglas Millward title: Software Engineering Project Management --- This module introduces students to the underpinning concepts and skills of Software Engineering Project Management, as well as introducing concepts of Behaviour Driven Development (BDD). This includes both the traditional tools of project management including estimating, planning and scheduling, as well as those of software architecture and engineering disciplines. The module describes requirements management, SDLC approaches, including continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD), as well as tools often delivered as part of the Agile methodology. Students will be introduced to the techniques in an engaging format, using a mixture of group work and individual activities.
