--- number: 3 name: Secure Software Development (Computer Science) type: Non-core slug: secure-software-development start_date: August 2021 end_date: October 2021 tutor: Dr. Cathryn Peoples title: Secure Software Development (Computer Science) --- This module introduces students to the underpinning concepts and skills of Secure Software Development. This includes a review of traditional and contemporary Software Development LifeCycle (SDLC)models, focusing in particular on the areas most affected by secure coding considerations. The course will provide students with a combination of an understanding of security principles together with exposure to automated testing tools. Students will be introduced to the techniques in an engaging format, using a mixture of practical exercises, group work and individual activities.
